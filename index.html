About me

Just another regular guy in the neighborhood. Always ready for being part of any positive activity. Giving his best performance and capabilities in school, friendship, family and work. Definitely not a hero saving the world, but a real model of a sincere, hardworking, family person.

The greatest strength includes being always optimistic, and giving his best to achieve his goals. So far, his special achievment includes being chosen as the Valedictorian of the SEE University (Tetovo, Northern Macedonia) during his bachelor studies for Business Informatics. He earned his master’s degree on Web Technologies at the Linneaus University (Vaxjo, Sweden).

Has been part of many community activities and NGOs, including the organization of youth competitions, grass-root activities and IT projects. The most influential project being the development of TechUP, a free programming course that has inspired and influenced the youth to continue studying IT.
